Biography

Welcome to my little corner of the internet. I'm a BCA student from India with a passion for technology, creativity, and continuous learning. Most of my time is spent building websites, experimenting with new programming concepts, and turning ideas into real projects. I enjoy the process of creating something from scratch and solving problems one step at a time. Beyond coding, I love discovering new music, playing games, exploring new technologies, and documenting the things that inspire me. This website is more than just a portfolio—it's a personal journal where I share what I'm currently working on, what I'm listening to, the projects I'm building, moments from college, and the experiences that shape my journey. Whether you're here to browse my projects, leave a message, or simply see what I'm up to, I hope you enjoy your visit. Thanks for stopping by!
